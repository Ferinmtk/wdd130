Overview

Purpose

Setting up an online presence for your rafting business can be a game-changer. A well-designed website can attract more customers by showcasing your trips, safety measures, and booking options. It also builds trust with potential customers and allows for easy online bookings. Sharing reviews and linking to social media can further enhance your reach. Checking out other rafting companies' websites can give you ideas on how to make yours stand out.

Audience

Our website targets adventurous individuals aged 18-45, seeking thrilling outdoor experiences. They are tech-savvy, environmentally conscious and value experiences over possessions.

Scenario Questions:

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on).
Our target customers are adventurous individuals aged 18-45, with a passion for outdoor activities. They are likely to have a moderate to high income and value experiences that offer excitement and adventure.

What do they want?
They want thrilling outdoor experiences that challenge them physically and mentally and provide lasting memories.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
They expect to find detailed information about our rafting experiences, including difficulty levels, safety measures and booking options. They also expect to find testimonials and information about our commitment to environmental sustainability.
